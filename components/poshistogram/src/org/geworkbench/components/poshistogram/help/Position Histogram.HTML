Position Histogram

The Position Histogram is located in the View Area in the upper right of the application. This component displays results from the Sequence Discovery Panel in the Analysis Area in the lower-right of the application. This plots the positions of SPLASH motifs as a Position Histograms.

Basic Usage

The steps of basic usage are indicated in the above screenshot.

A. Plot Position: Displays the motifs in the Sequence Panel as they are selected. Each motif is represented by a specific color which is the same in both panels.

B. Save Graph: Stores the graph created as a jpeg file.

C. Filter: Applies the filters of Avg/Peak and Abs/Rel.

D. ABS/Rel: Displays the fraction or absolute of total number of hits in the y axis. To apply, click Avg/Rel., Filter and Plot.

E. ABS/Peak: Alters step size to the sum of the p-value at every occurrence of the hit. To apply, click Abs/Peak, Filter and Plot.

F. This has not been implemented

G. This has not been implemented

H. Step: The step is the average of the motif histogram across positions. The lower the step size the more fine-grained motif is displayed.

I. Display: This is the display area of the graph which is stored when the graph is saved.

J. Support: The y axis is the number of sequence hits for that pattern.

K. Position: The x axis is the position on the sequence at which the hit occurs.

L. Legend: The legend for the sequences that is included in the position histogram.

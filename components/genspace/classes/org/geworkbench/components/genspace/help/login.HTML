genSpace: Login

To register with genSpace, log in, set your privacy settings, or log out of genSpace, choose "genSpace" from the geWorkbench "Tools" menu. Then choose the "genSpace Login" tab.

Registration
If this is your first time using genSpace, you can register by clicking the "Register" button. Then fill out the registration form and click "Save".

Logging In
Once you have registered, you can log in with your username and password. Only logged in users have the ability to change their privacy settings, or to rate and comment on analysis tools and workflows.

Privacy Settings
At any point while you are logged in, you can change the way in which collected data is associated with you. Just choose the "Data Visibility" tab. Analysis events (i.e., running an analysis tool) are logged so that the genSpace community can learn about analysis tool usage. You can choose to have these events logged with your username, anonymously, or not at all. Note that events are recorded anonymously if you are not logged in.

Editing Your Profile
You can edit your profile in the "General Profile" tab. Note that none of this data are shared with other genSpace users.

Logging Out
You can log out by clicking the "logout" button.

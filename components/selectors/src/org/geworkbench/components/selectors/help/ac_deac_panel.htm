Activate/Deactivate a Panel

Activated panels affect the contents displayed in the various visualization components. Most such components provide an option for imposing a"panel-view". When such a view takes effect, the component will only display a subset of the original data that contains those markers (microarrays)that are contained in activated marker panels (phenotypes).

An activated panel is available to other caWorkbench components. Unless it is activated, a panel will not be taken into consideration by other components. More than one panel can be active at the same time. Activated panels have red font to distinguish them from non-activated panels. Perform the following steps to deactivate a panel:

1 Click on the panel and the selected panel label will be highlighted in blue.

2 Activate a panel by performing one of the following actions:

。 Right-clicking on the panel and select Activate

。 Selecting the checkbox next to the panel name.

。 Through the main menu> Commands Panel > Activate Panel.

Deactivate

Deactivate is used to disable the activation of an already activated panel. The deactivated panel will not be available to other components. An activated panel cannot be deactivated other than by applying the Deactivate function. Perform the following steps to deactivate a panel:

1 Click on the panel and the selected panel label will be highlighted in blue.

2 Deactivate a panel by performing one of the following actions: Deactivate.
